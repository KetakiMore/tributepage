Neerja Bhanot

Neerja Bhanot, who sacrificed her life while protecting the lives of 359 passengers on Pan Am Flight 73 in 1986 when it was hijacked by a terrorist organization.

  • Died - 5 September 1986
  • Occupation - Flight purser, Model
